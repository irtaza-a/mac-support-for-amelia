MacBook Pro (Mid-2012, A1278) Project

Full refurbishment, hardware upgrade, and macOS restoration of a MacBook Pro, brought back to peak performance, gifted to my 8-year-old niece, with ongoing support provided.

Upgrades & Repairs

  • Installed WD Blue SATA SSD 1TB
  • Installed Kingston 2x8GB DDR3 1500 MHz SODIMMs
  • Replaced old A1322 battery with NinjaBatt replacement
  • Replaced MagSafe 1 port
  • Thorough internal cleaning including motherboard, with Isopropanol
  • Reapplied CPU thermal paste using Arctic MX-4
  • Purchased original A1184 L-style 60W MagSafe 1 charger
  • Replaced feet and back cover screws
  • macOS recovery & OCLP patching from Mountain Lion → Catalina → Monterey → Sonoma
  • Performance & thermal test analysis

Ongoing Support

This MacBook Pro is actively maintained and supported for smooth usage by my niece, ensuring software updates, system stability, and performance monitoring.
